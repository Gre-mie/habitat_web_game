Habitat

This game was created as a throw-away curiosity project. It explores storing information in the 'data' attribute of html elements.

How to play:

Start by picking the habitat you'll collinise. Each habitat has differnt amounts of resources, likelyhood of sickness and affects life expectancy

You'll start with a small population and can set what jobs workers can do each round. Any citizens of working age that are not assigned to a job have a chance to produce offspring. Children have a chance of gaining a work buff if theres at least one old person.

People will die of old age, sickness or starvation. Build houses to keep your citizens healthy.
