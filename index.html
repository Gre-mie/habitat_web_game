Habitat

This game was created as a throw-away curiosity project. It explores storing information in the 'data' attribute of html elements.

WARNING: This game may feel broken or unfair due to random being used without checks for fairness. For example, its possible to set all workers to gather one resource and recieve nothing. Its also possible for all citizens to become sick in the first turn if no shelter is built.

How to play:

Start by picking the habitat you'll collinise. Each habitat has differnt amounts of resources, likelyhood of sickness and affects death rates from old age

You'll start with a small population and can set what jobs workers can do each round. Any citizens of working age that are not assigned to a job have a chance to produce offspring. Children have a chance to grow into adults the more old people there are.

People will die of old age, sickness or starvation. Sickness affects the elderly, then children then adults. Sick citizens cant work or grow. Build houses to keep your citizens healthy.
